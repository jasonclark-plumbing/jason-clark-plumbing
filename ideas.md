# Jason Clark Plumbing — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Victorian Craftsman Gold
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Victorian Trade Card meets Modern Craft

**Core Principles:**
- Deep black backgrounds with rich gold accents, mirroring the business card exactly
- Serif and script typography to evoke heritage craftsmanship and trustworthiness
- Ornamental border details and decorative dividers as signature motifs
- Bold, confident layout with strong typographic hierarchy

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black (#0a0a0a, #111111)
- Gold: warm antique gold (#C9A84C, #D4AF37, #E8C96B)
- White: cream-white (#F5F0E8) for body text
- Accent: deep amber for hover states

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero with the logo centred on a dark textured background
- Asymmetric content sections alternating text-left/image-right
- Ornamental section dividers (thin gold lines with diamond centres)

**Signature Elements:**
- Circular badge motifs echoing the logo
- Thin gold border frames on cards and sections
- Decorative corner flourishes on key panels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states reveal gold underlines and subtle glow
- Smooth scroll with entrance fade-in animations
- CTA buttons with gold border that fills on hover

**Animation:**
- Fade-up entrance for section headings (200ms ease-out)
- Gold shimmer sweep on hero text
- Card hover lifts with subtle gold shadow

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (bold, italic for script feel)
- Body: Lora (serif, readable)
- Accent labels: Montserrat (small caps, letter-spaced)
</text>

## Approach 2: Industrial Blueprint
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Blueprint / Technical Precision

**Core Principles:**
- Dark navy/charcoal with gold highlights on a grid-textured background
- Monospace and geometric sans-serif typography for technical authority
- Blueprint-style grid lines and measurement annotations as decorative elements
- Structured, grid-heavy layout with clear information hierarchy

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: deep navy (#0D1B2A)
- Gold: bright metallic (#FFD700)
- Grid lines: subtle blue-grey (#1E3A5F)
- Text: off-white (#E8EDF2)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Sidebar navigation on desktop, full-width mobile
- Content in defined grid cells with visible dividers
- Services displayed as blueprint-style technical cards

**Signature Elements:**
- Blueprint grid texture on hero and section backgrounds
- Measurement tick marks and annotation lines
- Pipe/wrench icon set in a technical line-art style

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Click interactions feel precise and mechanical
- Tooltips appear on service icons with technical specs
- Form fields styled as blueprint input boxes

**Animation:**
- Draw-on line animations for section borders
- Counter animations for stats (years experience, jobs completed)
- Blueprint grid fades in behind hero text

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk (geometric, technical)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans (clean, readable)
- Code/labels: JetBrains Mono
</text>

## Approach 3: Heritage Signage (CHOSEN)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Heritage British Trade Signage — the aesthetic of classic hand-painted shop signs, gold-leaf lettering, and Victorian-era trade cards

**Core Principles:**
- Jet black backgrounds with layered gold tones (matte, satin, bright)
- Script and serif typography that feels hand-crafted and authoritative
- Decorative ornamental elements (rules, flourishes, badges) used sparingly but deliberately
- Generous whitespace within sections to let the gold breathe against the black

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary background: #0C0C0C (near-black, warm)
- Section alternates: #111111 and #161616
- Gold primary: #C9A84C (antique, matte)
- Gold bright: #E8C96B (highlights, hover)
- Gold dark: #8B6914 (shadows, depth)
- Text: #F0EAD6 (warm cream, not pure white)
- Muted text: #9A8A6A

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero with centred ornamental badge layout
- Services in a staggered asymmetric grid (not uniform columns)
- Contact section as a dark card with gold border frame
- Sticky top navigation bar with gold rule underline

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin double-rule gold dividers between sections
- Circular badge/seal motifs for credentials and trust signals
- Ornamental corner brackets on featured panels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: gold colour intensifies, subtle upward translate
- CTA buttons: solid gold fill with black text, border expands on hover
- Navigation links: gold underline slides in from left on hover

**Animation:**
- Section entrance: fade-up with 60ms stagger per element
- Hero: logo badge scales from 0.95 to 1.0 on load
- Scroll-triggered counter animations for stats

**Typography System:**
- Display/Hero: Playfair Display (bold, italic variants)
- Subheadings: Playfair Display SC (small caps)
- Body: Lora (serif, warm and readable)
- Labels/Nav: Montserrat (uppercase, tracked, 500 weight)
</text>

</response>
